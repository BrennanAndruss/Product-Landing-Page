About Us

Cal Poly Distance Club (CPDC) is the official running club of Cal Poly SLO. From the most casual to most competitive runners, the Cal Poly Distance Club is a great space to challenge oneself and establish community!

Races in California and beyond!

Members have the opportunity to compete with other clubs and teams across the nation. We race cross country in the Fall and track in Spring. CPDC has earned a reputation of high achievement in the National Intercollegiate Running Club Association (NIRCA). More than just racing!

Members enjoy access to social events, which are a great opportunity to form close bonds within the club. Some of the most popular events include adventure runs, CPDC Formal, The Pizza Run, and an annual camping trip. Work hard, play harder!

How to Join

There is a two week trial period for CPDC, so feel free to come out to practice and see how you like it! Practices are Monday-Friday at 4:10pm on the Cal Poly track (50m mark). Once the two week trial period is up, you will have to join the club if you wish to continue coming to practice!

If you decide to join...

  • Go to Cal Poly Now and sign in using your Cal Poly login
  • Go to Organizations and search for "Distance Club"
  • Click the "Join" button
  • Sign the participation agreement

Paying club dues

Club dues are $60 (this covers the whole year and includes social events, a club t-shirt, and personalized coaching from Coach Mando). After paying dues, you will be able to attend practices all year long and compete in races (optional) as a Cal Poly Distance Club athlete.

You can pay with either cash or check. Bring it to practice and give it to the CPDC treasurer. Please bring an envelope and remember to write your name on it!
